    We’ve had the pleasure of working on projects which have been seen around the world, and some which have just been born.

    The Brief.

    Design-led gift retailer Wrapped! requested a logo and brand identity for their luxury gift shop, due to launch alongside Canterbury Cathedral. The logo needed to reflect the brand’s luxury appeal, and be versatile for use as store signage, on bags and instore merchandise. The double ‘P’ in the logo cleverly mimics a present and a pair of people.
